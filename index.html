📖 How GPA is calculated (as per R.7.10)

GPA = Σ(Cᵢ × GPᵢ) / ΣCᵢ where ΣCᵢ includes credits for courses with F, W, L grades (even though they carry 0 grade points). Courses with I (Incomplete) and U (Audit) are completely excluded from both numerator and denominator.

This calculator follows the exact relative grading rules used in IITs/IIITs. Enter your course credits and grades, then add any previous CGPA to compute your overall cumulative CGPA.

💡 Example: A 5-credit course with grade 'B' (GP=8) contributes 40 quality points and 5 denominator credits. An 'F' grade contributes 0 points but still adds 5 denominator credits – which lowers your GPA.
